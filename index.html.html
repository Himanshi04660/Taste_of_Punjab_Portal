Who We Are

Punjabi Tradition with Modern Hospitality.

Perched atop Powai with stunning skyline views, Taste of Punjab is a rooftop restaurant that offers authentic Punjabi cuisine, vibrant ambience, and a well-stocked bar — the perfect setting for both relaxed meals and festive occasions. Our rooftop banquet, Antariksh, provides an elegant venue for tying the knot or hosting celebrations under the open sky.

Rooted in the richness of Punjabi culture, our team is committed to creating seamless and memorable experiences for every guest. From private parties to festive gatherings, we ensure every detail is thoughtfully executed. Our expert chefs serve vibrant, traditional dishes with a contemporary twist, complemented by a welcoming atmosphere and personalised service.
